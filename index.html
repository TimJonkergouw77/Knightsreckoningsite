Defeat Goblins

The goal is to eliminate all goblins in the area. These mischievous creatures are causing chaos, and it’s your mission to stop them. Use your weapons, spells, and strategy to defeat them before they overrun the land. Be cautious—goblins can be tricky and attack in groups. Collect rewards and power-ups as you progress, and don’t let your guard down. Victory is achieved when every goblin is defeated. Prepare for a thrilling battle and save the realm!

Take back your territory

The goal is to reclaim the towers that have been taken over by the goblins. These mischievous creatures have seized control of important areas, and it's your mission to take back what is rightfully yours. Using strategy, weapons, and courage, you must fight to regain each tower, one by one. As you defeat goblins and reclaim your territory, the land will slowly be freed from their chaos. Prepare for battle and restore peace to the realm!

Buy Perks

After each level, purchase powerful perks to boost health, speed, and special skills. Strategize upgrades, collect resources, and become stronger to face tougher goblins. Your choices lead to victory!
